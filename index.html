Hi!

Thanks for stopping by my storytelling portfolio. Here, you'll find a collection of my personal storytelling projects, life gesture drawing, and artwork. If you'd like to see more, feel free to check out my social media!

I’m a Storyboard Artist who likes turning first ideas of the script into drawings. I enjoy the sketching part, where the story starts to grow, and I love trying to make people feel emotions through each shot.
