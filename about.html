Carlos Carpe is a passionate Story Artist who has been learning and growing in the world of film and animation since 2021. His work stands out for using thumbnails as a key tool to bring ideas to life. He truly believes that a good story can create real emotions in the audience — and that’s what he aims for from the very first sketch.

He studied 3D Animation at ESI Murcia, but soon realized that his real passion was building stories from the ground up. This led him to specialize in storyboarding with a Master’s degree at UTAD (Madrid), where he deepened his knowledge of visual storytelling, cinematography, and character-driven scenes.

Driven by a strong desire to keep growing both as an artist and a person, Carlos observes his surroundings with sensitivity, always looking for emotions and moments to turn into honest and expressive stories. When he’s not drawing, he’s probably playing padel or watching a film to keep feeding his creative vision.
